Røros → Flåm

Shielings and wilderness: gravel from the UNESCO mining town across Rondane and Dovre, onto Mjølkevegen's high pastures, then down the Rallarvegen to the fjord. Two riders, tents, and a decision to make at Dombås.

Decide on the day: the route crosses a railway at Røros, Alvdal, Dombås, Gol, Geilo, Haugastøl, Finse, Myrdal and Flåm — you can join late or bail early at any of them.
