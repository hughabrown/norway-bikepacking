Røros → Flåm

Train to Dombås, then gravel across the wild-reindeer plateaus onto Mjølkevegen's summer-farm country and down the Rallarvegen to the fjord at Flåm. Home the scenic way: express boat down the Sognefjord to Bergen, then the Bergensbanen back to Oslo.

Fri 10 → Thu 16 riding (avg ~75 km/day) · Fri 17 Flåm→Bergen ferry · Sat 18 Bergen→Oslo train · fly out Sun 19. Pick your flavour above.

Packing

Pack for 5–20 °C, rain that will find you, near-20-hour daylight and mosquitoes on the plateaus; Finse nights can dip to ~3 °C. Red on the bike = still to buy. Two rear panniers (≈40 L) plus the red bags ≈ 55 L per rider — enough.
